How to Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to have your data deleted. If you wish to request the deletion of your account and all associated information from Gisty, please follow the instructions below.

Deletion Request Process

To request the deletion of your account and data, please send an email to the developer with your request:

Email Details:

To: anas.kala@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Message: Include a brief description of your request to delete your account and associated data.

Once we receive your deletion request, we will process it promptly and confirm the deletion of your data.

What Gets Deleted

Upon your request, we will ensure that:

  • All your account information is removed
  • Any associated data is permanently deleted
  • No backups or records of your data are retained

Response Time

We aim to process all account deletion requests within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been successfully deleted.

Questions?
